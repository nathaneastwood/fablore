Oscilio, Constella Intelligence, was created by the Aetherscribes of the past to protect the future.

Together, the scribes imbued Oscilio with their wisdom; shaped him to withstand the cataclysm that followed the war of the Third Age between the Ancients and the Old Ones.

And now, Oscilio has awoken, and he has a warning for all of Rathe.

He will share the knowledge of the Aetherscribes with those who would use it to rebuild the strength of Enion. He will seek the signs of the Old Ones’ return, ever observant of the subtle disturbances and echoes that might signal their resurgence. He will ensure that Rathe is ready to face its uncertain future.

But first, Oscilio must move with a purpose as ancient as the stones beneath his feet whilst navigating the eccentricities of Aria’s diverse inhabitants.

Oscilio may be the aetheric embodiment of infinite wisdom, designed to defend against an ancient threat, but he has much to learn about the Aria of today.

# Oscilio, Forked Continuum

Oscilio is a constellation of sacrifices made near the end of the Third Age, purpose-built to endure what his creators could not.

His function was once simple. He would preserve what the Aetherscribes knew, and he would warn Rathe of what might come. Now, time in Aria has changed him, and the omens hanging over the region are testing the very limits of what he was made to do.

Reforged by Lightning into the Forked Continuum, Oscilio’s prime objective now is to find the cause of the omens and to mend the breaches and staunch the corruption that now bleeds into Aria, before it’s too late.

While Oscilio may have outwardly changed, his resolve remains constant. He will use his knowledge to study the threat and amplify his assault upon the unknown. And he is not alone. The Aetherscribes are more than imprints in his consciousness, they are a felt presence, and they are sending cosmic clues to help guide Oscilio’s decisions.

Calm as the eye of the storm, Oscilio will command the Lightning and rouse Aria against the dark times to come.
