Zyggy Starlight projects vivid holos across unimaginable borders, warning Rathe of ominous troubles ahead.

Before they found themself exiled to the Nebulus Rift, Zyggy was an Aetherscribe Illusionist of the Third Age. During the formation of Valahai, they crafted powerful holos for the war effort. Within the Auric Keep, at the heart of Valahai, Zyggy helped build the Constella Intelligence known as Oscilio.

Then the Ancients sacrificed themselves to stop the Old Ones.

In that blinding cataclysm, Zyggy raised a holographic shield that spared the keep from annihilation. But the Ancients’ sacrifice came at a price. The keep was flung into the Nebulus Rift, a strange starlit realm where time and matter roil in constant flux.

Centuries of cosmic exile have fractured Zyggy’s identity, and imbued them with an intense concentration of Lightning. Crystalline-clad and wise beyond mortal understanding, Zyggy now sees what is, what was, and what might be, all fates flickering like starlight above the Auric towers.
